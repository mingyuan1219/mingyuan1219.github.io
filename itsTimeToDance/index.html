About

This is a song written for my younger sister at the end of 2023. It takes me over two months to record and produce the song, and I’ve finally published it on 1/16/2024. It was not easy and it’s still not perfect, since I’m not a singer (while my sister is actually a great one with the most beautiful voice in this world), but I really enjoy this experience.

And to my sister - you know what this is for…you know I will always love you.
